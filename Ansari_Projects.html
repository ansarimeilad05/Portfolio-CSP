For this project, we were supposed to make an app that could have been a game or a trivia type of app. So, my partner and I Sanjana J decided that we are going to do a trivia app where we can ask the user's company logos pictures and they have to guess what the logo. There was a 10-sec timer. When the 10 sec is up, it would make a sad trombone sound. This project took 2-3 weeks to finish. We had a beta and final walk to show our peers what we have done and get some feedback. We got good feedback from our class and some improvement feedback. From this project, my partner and I learned how team-work and design/code can make a functional app. This project was assigned on September 23 and due on October 5. The Mini Beta Gallery Walk: 09/30. The Project app Deadline: 10/05 The Final Gallery Walk: 10/08. In this project, I have learned that working with a group could be less stressful and they are your group mates that can help you when needed. One challenge that I and my partner faced is that our timer didn't count down from 10 to 0 then it would make a sad trombone sound. I learned as a creator that the stress and the fun you go while making an app.
